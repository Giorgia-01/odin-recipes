Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of layered pasta, meat sauce, besciamella, and cheese, baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • Besciamella
  • Fresh egg pasta sheets
  • 100g grated Parmigiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce
  3. Add a layer of pasta sheets
  4. Cover with meat sauce, then spoon over some besciamella
  5. Sprinkle with Parmigiano
  6. Repeat layers (pasta, sauces, cheese) until ingredients are used up, finishing with besciamella and Parmigiano
  7. Bake for about 30–40 minutes, until golden and bubbling
  8. Let rest 10–15 minutes before serving
