Tiramisù

Description

Tiramisù is a classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, topped with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 400g Ladyfingers
  • 500g Mascarpone cheese
  • 300ml Coffee
  • 120g Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • Bitter cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until creamy and light. Add mascarpone cheese and stir until homogeneous.
  2. In another bowl beat egg whiteswith a pinch of salt until firm and incorporate them to mascarpone cream.
  3. Prepare coffee and let it chill. Dip quickly ladyfinger in the coffee one by one and lay them in a casserole.
  4. Cover them with some cream and repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients. Finish with the cream.
  5. Finish the Tiramisù with the cocoa powder. Let the dessert sit in the fridge for at least 4 hours.
